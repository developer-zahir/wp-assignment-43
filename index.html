Sea Nymphs Suites

Combining architecture with mythology, Erato and Calypso are two of our distinct acquisitions, offering the visitor a sense of luxury and sensitivity to detail, each Suite highlighting the power of austerity by enlivening the earthly elements that make up it.

The Nirrians (Nymphs of the Sea), according to the transfer of mythology, were fifty, although some of them want to reach the hundred. They lived on the seabed in the palace of their father, Nireas. They had the power to roam the sea

but to soothe it whenever they wanted. Thanks to their immortality, they accompanied the chariots of the gods of the sea. The Nirrians did not allow mortals to compete in beauty and charm. Traditions want to spend their time dancing and swimming dolphins. Again, they show them thorns or rocks restored to sing, weave or care for their long hair.
